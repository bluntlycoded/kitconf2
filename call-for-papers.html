Call for Papers

We invite researchers, academics, and industry professionals to submit original research papers on sustainable business technologies and green innovation for presentation at the Green Innovation Conference 2025.

Submission Deadline: October 15, 2025
IEEE Format Required

Papers are invited on innovative research and development in the following areas related to sustainable business and green technology.

Sustainable Manufacturing

  • Green production technologies
  • Waste reduction strategies
  • Eco-friendly materials
  • Industry 4.0 for sustainability

Renewable Energy Systems

  • Solar and wind technologies
  • Energy storage solutions
  • Smart grid integration
  • Microgrids and distributed energy

Circular Economy

  • Resource optimization
  • Waste-to-resource technologies
  • Product lifecycle management
  • Sharing economy models

Green Finance & Investment

  • ESG investment strategies
  • Green bonds and financing
  • Carbon markets
  • Sustainable business models

AI for Sustainability

  • Optimization algorithms

Sustainable Supply Chain

  • Green logistics
  • Supplier sustainability
  • Carbon footprint reduction
  • Transparency and traceability

Submission Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines carefully to ensure your submission is considered for review.

Paper Format

Length Requirement

Maximum 8 pages including references and appendices

Format Standard

IEEE two-column conference format (A4 size)

Language

Papers must be written in English

File Format

Submit as PDF format only

Template Download: IEEE conference paper template available at ieee.org/templates

Review Process

Double-Blind Peer Review

Each paper reviewed by at least 3 experts

Review Criteria

Originality, technical quality, relevance, and clarity

Review Timeline

4-6 weeks from submission deadline

Publication

Accepted papers published in IEEE Xplore

Best Paper Awards: Outstanding papers will receive recognition and prizes
