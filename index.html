About Kalam Institute of Technology

Kalam Institute of Technology (KIT), Berhampur stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education and innovation. Named after the visionary Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, our institute embodies his spirit of scientific inquiry and technological advancement.

Established with the mission to nurture tomorrow's engineers and innovators, KIT has consistently been at the forefront of promoting sustainable technology solutions and green innovation in education and research.
