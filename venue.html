Detailed Directions

From Berhampur Railway Station

Take NH-16 towards Bhubaneswar for 12 km. Turn right at KIT signboard. The auditorium is located in the main campus building.

From Bhubaneswar Airport

Take NH-16 southbound for approximately 180 km. Exit at Berhampur and follow signs to KIT campus. Journey time: ~3.5 hours.

Local Transportation

Auto-rickshaws and buses are available from Berhampur city center. Taxi services can be arranged through the conference team.
