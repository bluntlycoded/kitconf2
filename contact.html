Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and the conference.

What's the best way to contact you for urgent matters?

For urgent matters, please call our main office at +91-680-2345678 during office hours (9 AM - 6 PM IST, Monday-Friday). For after-hours emergencies, send an email to info@greeninnovation2025.kit.ac.in with "URGENT" in the subject line.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Paper submission queries are prioritized and usually answered within 24 hours. For faster responses, please use the specific departmental email addresses listed above.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes, our office is open for visitors during regular business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST; Saturday, 9 AM - 2 PM IST). We recommend calling ahead to ensure the appropriate team member is available to assist you. Our office is located at KIT Campus, Berhampur, Odisha.

Do you have social media accounts for updates?

Yes, follow us on our social media channels for regular updates, announcements, and behind-the-scenes content. Links to our official social media accounts are available in the footer of this website. We post daily updates during the conference period.
